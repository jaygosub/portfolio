The Brief

Redesigning a regiment

The Coldstream Guards badge carries centuries of history — the star, the motto, the Garter. The challenge was to honour that legacy while pushing the vector craft to its absolute limit.

Every element was hand-drawn in Adobe Illustrator. No live trace, no shortcuts — just anchors, handles and patience.

Vector detail — Adobe Illustrator

The Render

From flat to photorealistic

Once the vector was complete, the artwork was brought into a 3D pipeline. AI-assisted rendering breathes light and material into the flat geometry — metal, enamel, gold leaf.

The result sits somewhere between archive photography and product shot.

Final photorealistic render — AI assisted
